Description — Edit

This scene highlights layers for Portland, Oregon available in ArcGIS to support your work in 3D. Use these layers in conjunction with your own layers to create new scenes focused on a specific topic or area of interest to you. What is a scene? Think of it as a map for working in 3D but with additional settings for things like sun angle.

What's in this scene?
1) Terrain: includes a global 3D terrain layer to provide elevation context. Your layers are placed in relationship to this terrain
2) Basemap: includes one of the ArcGIS Basemaps regularly used in in your mapping work
3) Scene Layers: includes a layer of 3D buildings to help understand your data within the context of the built environment. The layer is a file type optimized for rendering in 3D.

This scene has pop ups available for the Buildings layer to provide useful information


Create your own scene:
1) Sign in with your ArcGIS account and create new scene
2) Add this scene: Click ADD LAYERS and click Import

3) Choose basemap: Select one of the ArcGIS basemaps from the Basemap Gallery
4) Add your own unique layers (Note: publishing multipatch features like 3D buildings will be supported in the 10.3.1 release of ArcGIS)
5) Create slides to direct users to interesting places in your scene - See More
6) Save and share the results of your work with others in your organization and the public
