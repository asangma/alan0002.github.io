Body MetRx - Website

Designing the user interface for the Body MetRx website began with an understanding of what Body MetRx does, who they would do it for, and why they do it. Clarity is key when designing any user interface.

The Goal

Design and build a mobile-first website that will clearly communicate offered services and prices, allow users to schedule appointments, and assist clients in being prepared for their appointments.
