Inland Empire Zine Quest - Logo

Inland Empire Zine Quest is a locally hosted fair where the focus is on self-published comic books, art books, and of course magazines. It is an event which encourages creative cross polination and networking.

The Goal

Design a logo that communicates the message of “creatively seeking abroad and forging forward” to the broader Inland Empire community. The aesthetic had to be informal but professional. It also had to align with the host's brand (A Shop Called Quest).

Required Skills
  • Consultation
  • Brand Identity
  • Press-ready File Creation
