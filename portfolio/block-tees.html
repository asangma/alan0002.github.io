Block CrossFit T-Shirts

Block CrossFit is a CrossFit gym where the coaches are serious about fitness but not about themselves. Giving their clients the best experience is central to their coaching style. Their motto is “Forging elite fitness...and high fives!”

The Goal

Design t-shirt graphics that communicate a dedication to fitness and fun in equal parts.

Required Skills
  • Graphic Design
  • Illustration
  • Coordination with Screen Printer
